65. Count teachers born in each year.

 Part 10: HAVING Clause Exercises

67. Display gender groups having more than 20 teachers.
    select gender,count(*) as total_teachers from teacher group by gender having count(*)>20

    

68. Display birth years having more than 3 teachers.
    SELECT YEAR(dob) AS birth_year, COUNT(*) AS total_teacher 
    FROM teacher 
    GROUP BY YEAR(dob) 
    HAVING COUNT(*) > 3

69. Display gender groups whose average weight exceeds 70 kg.
    SELECT gender, AVG(weight) AS average_weight
    FROM teacher
    GROUP BY gender
    HAVING AVG(weight) > 70
 
70. Display groups where total weight exceeds 1000 kg.
    SELECT gender, SUM(weight) AS total_weight
    FROM teacher
    GROUP BY gender
    HAVING SUM(weight) > 1000
